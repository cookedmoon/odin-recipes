Dino Nuggets

Descripton

Dino nuggs are a staple of the diet of college kids around the world. Quick, easy, affordable, and delicious there is nothing else I would prefer to have on a Thursday night after a long coding session.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Dino Nuggets
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Set air fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Put dino nuggs in for 10-15 minutes or until desired doneness
  3. remove from air fryer and let cool for 2 minutes before eating
