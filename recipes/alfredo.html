Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Ever want to make a week night into date night? Ever give up thinking it's too hard and too complicated and that you don't have the skills? This recipe will change that. May I introduce a recipe that is adored by millions, loved by most, and eaten by all. Fettuccine Alfredo made at home in just minutes. It really is that simple.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs. Fresh fettuccine
  • 2 cups cream
  • 1/2 cup Reggiano Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water according to the manufacturer's instruction
  2. In a saute pan, add the cream and bring it to a boil. Add the Reggiano and bring the mixture to boil unil it thickens enough to coat the back of your sppon. This may take a few minutes. When thick enough add the drained pasta, mix well to coat the pasta, plate immediately.
  3. Add more cheese and black pepper and serve immediately!
