Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Ever want to make a week night into date night? Ever give up thinking it's too hard and too complicated and that you don't have the skills? This recipe will change that. May I introduce a recipe that is adored by millions, loved by most, and eaten by all. Fettuccine Alfredo made at home in just minutes. It really is that simple.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs. Fresh fettuccine
  • 2 cups cream
  • 1/2 cup Reggiano Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
