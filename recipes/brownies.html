Brownies

Description

This recipe will change you life. Nothing is better than a gooey perfectly chocolaty brownie to wrap up a night of passionate studying. Making a delicious brownie will transform your cooking game.

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons salted butter melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup melted milk chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Line a metal 9x9 pan with parchment paper
  3. Pour melted butter into a large mixing bowl. Whisk in sugar by and until smooth, 30 seconds
  4. Add in eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk 1 minute
  5. Whisk in melted chocolate until combined and smooth
  6. Use a rubber spatula to stir in flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined. Stir in whole chocolate chips
  7. Pour into prepared pan and smooth out
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes
  9. Let cool in pan for 30 minutes before slicing
